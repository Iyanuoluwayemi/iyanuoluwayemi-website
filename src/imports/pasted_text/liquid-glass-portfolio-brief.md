UI/UX Design Brief: Premium "Liquid Glass" Designer Portfolio

1. Project Overview

Client: Iyanuoluwa Oluwayemi (Visual Identity & Social Media Designer)
Goal: Design a highly converting, ultra-minimalist, single-page portfolio. The goal is to build instant trust, exude a premium "high-end agency" feel, and showcase ROI-focused design.
Inspiration/Vibe: Apple WWDC25 "Liquid Glass", Framer templates, ultra-premium dark mode, refractive glassmorphism, cinematic lighting.

2. Art Direction & Styling

Theme: Dark Mode, sleek, spacious, and cinematic.

Background Style: The base background is almost pitch black. Overlaid on this are massive, heavily blurred, glowing "orbs" using the brand colors.

UI Elements: All containers, cards, and buttons (except primary CTAs) should utilize a "Liquid Glass" effect. They are semi-transparent, blurring the glowing orbs behind them.

Typography: Geist. Very tight tracking (letter-spacing) on massive headings.

3. Color Palette

Base Background: #050505 (Almost Black)

Primary Accent / Neon: #BFFE5F (Vibrant Lime/Neon Green) - Use for primary buttons, text highlights, and glowing background orbs.

Secondary Deep Accent: #02403D (Deep Rich Green) - Use exclusively for massive background glowing orbs and the text color inside the primary Lime buttons.

Text Colors: * Primary Text: #FFFFFF

Secondary Text: #A1A1AA (Zinc-400)

Glass Fill: #141416 at 40% - 50% opacity.

4. The "Liquid Glass" Design System (Crucial)

To achieve the Apple-esque refractive look, all cards/containers must follow these exact Figma properties:

Fill: Solid color #141416 (Dark Grey/Black) set to 40% Opacity.

Background Blur: 32px (This is critical for the fluid background to bend through the cards).

Inner Border (Stroke): 1px inside, Solid White #FFFFFF at 5% Opacity.

Drop Shadow: Y: 4, Blur: 24, Color: #000000 at 30%.

Inner Shadow (Top edge highlight): Y: 1, Blur: 1, Color: #FFFFFF at 10%.

Corner Radius: High radiuses. Use 32px (2rem) for large cards and full pill-shapes (999px) for buttons.

6. Prototyping & Interaction Notes (For the Designer)

Hover states: When hovering over a Liquid Glass card, the border should subtly shift to Neon Green at 30% opacity, and the card should scale up by 2%.

Image Hover: Portfolio images should have a subtle zoom-in effect on hover, and an Arrow icon should fade into the center of the image.
